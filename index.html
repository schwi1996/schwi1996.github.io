About Me


I am currently pursuing the Computer Systems Technology Diploma at BCIT.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Biochemistry from UBC, where I gained diverse professional experience. My roles ranged from office administration to customer service in the food industry and quality assurance testing. These positions honed my skills in bilingual translation (Mandarin and English), customer relations, and team coordination.

After completing my degree, I worked full-time at SGS, an agricultural laboratory, while managing a part-time role as a lifeguard and instructor at the local pool, alongside continuing education at UBC.

I am eager to embrace new learning opportunities, such as my current focus on Computer Science, and I value each chance to grow professionally.
